About Us

Etravel is an Indian online travel company founded in 2000. Headquartered in Gurugram, Haryana, the company provides online travel services including airline tickets, domestic and international holiday packages, hotel reservations, rail, and bus tickets. As of June 2023, the company has 146 active franchisees operating in over 100 cities.[4].Etravel also has international offices located in New York, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, Phuket, Bangkok, and Dubai.[5]
